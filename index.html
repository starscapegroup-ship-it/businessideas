Choosing a Business Idea Is the Hardest Part

Most people don’t fail because they lack motivation. They fail because they pick a business that doesn’t fit their situation — and the mismatch shows up later as stress, churn, and stalled progress.

Common mistakes

  • Not enough time to execute consistently
  • Underestimating startup costs and ramp time
  • Following trends without understanding operations
  • Skill mismatch (or no clear advantage)
